Today, I continue to develop my skills and deepen my passion for programming, constantly learning and evolving in this ever-changing field.

My Smart Home Project with Home Assistant

I am transforming my home into a smart living space using Home Assistant, an open-source automation platform. I run Home Assistant on a Raspberry Pi 3, which serves as the central hub for managing and automating my smart devices. My goal is to seamlessly integrate and control various devices in my two-story house, with a strong focus on automating my blinds and optimizing energy efficiency.

Technologies I Use

Raspberry Pi 3

The core of my smart home setup is a Raspberry Pi 3. It provides a lightweight yet powerful platform for running Home Assistant while ensuring flexibility for future upgrades. Despite its limited hardware resources, it handles automation tasks efficiently.

Zigbee Dongle

To create a reliable and energy-efficient smart home network, I use a Zigbee dongle. Zigbee's mesh capabilities allow me to connect and control devices such as sensors, lights, and other automation components without relying on Wi-Fi, making my setup more stable and responsive.

EnOcean Dongle

My blinds utilize EnOcean technology, which operates on energy harvesting, eliminating the need for batteries. By integrating an EnOcean dongle with Home Assistant, I can automate my blinds based on real-time conditions such as sunlight levels and schedules.

Challenges I Face

One of the biggest challenges I am facing is achieving precise control over my blinds. They use the UP-Jalousieaktor 1-Kanal 6A UPJ230/01 actuator, which requires careful calibration and compatibility adjustments within Home Assistant. I am actively testing different configurations to ensure smooth operation and responsiveness.

Future Plans

  • Enhance the reliability of the EnOcean integration by refining automation rules and improving device response times.
  • Expand my smart home ecosystem by incorporating additional Zigbee devices, such as motion sensors, smart plugs, and security cameras.
  • Implement advanced automation scenarios, including voice control, adaptive shading based on indoor temperature, and geofencing for energy optimization.

This project is an exciting opportunity for me to deepen my understanding of IoT technologies and bridge different communication protocols into a unified home automation system.

My Public & Internal Portfolio

I am currently working on a portfolio website that serves two main purposes:

  • A public portfolio to showcase my personal projects, skills, and experience.
  • An internal portfolio specifically designed for my workplace, allowing restricted access to work-related projects and documentation.

Design & Aesthetic

One of the key aspects of my portfolio is the interactive JavaScript-based background, featuring a starfield effect that creates a futuristic and dynamic look. This background, combined with a clean and modern UI, gives the portfolio a sleek and professional feel while making it visually engaging.

I want the public version to be easily accessible and act as a personal showcase for potential employers, collaborators, and anyone interested in my work. Meanwhile, the internal version will be restricted to my workplace, containing confidential projects and work-related content.

Technical Details & Features

  • App-Style Project Display – Projects are represented as app icons, keeping the interface clean and intuitive.
  • Secure Authentication – The internal portfolio will require a login system to ensure restricted access.
  • JavaScript & Animation Effects – Smooth transitions, modals for project details, and the starry background effect bring a polished feel to the site.
  • Responsive Design – Optimized for both desktop and mobile viewing.

This portfolio project is an exciting challenge as it merges front-end interactivity, security considerations, and user-friendly navigation into a cohesive platform. I'm continuously refining it to make both the public and internal versions as seamless and visually appealing as possible.
